Summary

The analysis prioritizes enzyme activities shared by human and parasite datasets but assigned to parasite cluster configurations absent from the human cluster set. These are reported as AnEnPi-reconstructed analog candidates and ranked for drug-target follow-up.

Data integrity: SUPERFAMILY and SCOP assignment tables were not present in the uploaded package. Fold fields are validation targets, not invented results.
