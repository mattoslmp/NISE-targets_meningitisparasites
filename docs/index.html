Project summary

This page summarizes a comparative enzymology analysis for meningitis-associated and neurologically relevant helminths. The workflow identifies enzyme activities shared by human and parasite datasets but assigned to parasite cluster configurations absent from the human cluster set. These records are reported as AnEnPi-reconstructed analog candidates and are prioritized for drug-target follow-up.

Data integrity: SUPERFAMILY and SCOP assignment tables were not present in the uploaded package. Fold fields are included as validation targets and are not invented results.

Main results

Dataset coverage
Angiostrongylus cantonensis genome and transcriptome, Echinococcus granulosus, Echinococcus multilocularis, Taenia solium, Toxocara canis and Onchocerca volvulus.
Core analog layer
26 EC activities were present as AnEnPi-reconstructed analog candidates in all seven datasets.
Score formula
Priority score = 2 x positive datasets + evidence bonus + EC-quality bonus + sequence-support bonus.
Best journal fit
The manuscript is organized for Parasites and Vectors as a computational parasitology and post-genomic resource article.
